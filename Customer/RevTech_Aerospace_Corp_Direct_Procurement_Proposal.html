Section 01

Executive Summary

In response to Solicitation No. AERO‑RFP‑KB‑03262026, Revelation Technologies proposes an engagement to transition The Aerospace Corporation's Direct Procurement execution from SAP Ariba into SAP S/4HANA, establishing S/4 as the authoritative system of execution for all direct procurement activity.

The Aerospace Corporation currently executes direct procurement workflows through SAP Ariba, requiring a layer of custom integrations between Ariba and S/4HANA to manage PR-to-PO handoffs. This customization introduces technical debt, elevates sustainment costs, and creates an integration surface area that is increasingly difficult to maintain as both platforms evolve.

This engagement consolidates direct procurement into SAP S/4HANA, including PR entry, PR approval, and PR-to-PO processing, eliminating the custom Ariba-to-S/4 integration layer and aligning Aerospace's procurement architecture to SAP-recommended standard functionality. The scope encompasses S/4HANA configuration, Dassian Contract Flow Down implementation, workflow enablement, integration updates across EPIC, ServiceNow, Metrology API, and Ariba systems, end-to-end testing, training materials development, and two months of post go-live hyper care support.

This proposal offers two commercial structures for The Aerospace Corporation's consideration:

Option A - Time & Materials ($394,710): Billed on actual hours consumed at discounted project rates. Provides maximum flexibility with cost based on effort delivered.

Option B - Firm Fixed Price ($454,667): Milestone‑based payments tied to deliverable acceptance at each phase gate. Includes a 15% risk contingency and provides budget certainty. Payment is contingent upon completion and formal acceptance of each phase's defined deliverables.

Both options include a complimentary Principal Solution Architect (216 hours, valued at $48,600) at no charge, a $5,000 credit toward SAP Ariba Shared Services support, and - contingent upon proposal acceptance - a complimentary Enterprise AI Evaluation and Assessment (80 hours, valued at $18,000) led by the Principal Solution Architect. Total complimentary value: $71,600. The engagement timeline runs April 13 through December 14, with a planned fiscal blackout during August and September, followed by two months of hyper care support.

Rate Condition: The discounted consulting rates reflected in this proposal are contingent upon The Aerospace Corporation's commitment to a minimum of three (3) sustainment resources under the separately proposed Sustainment / Staff Augmentation engagement provided to Aerospace on March 26, 2026.

Revelation Technologies brings deep SAP S/4HANA and Aerospace & Defense domain expertise to this engagement. Our team has executed procurement, supply chain, and ERP transformations for organizations operating under comparable regulatory and compliance frameworks. We understand the operational sensitivity of this transition and have structured the delivery to minimize disruption while maximizing process integrity.

Section 02

Business Objectives & Value

This transformation addresses architectural, operational, and financial imperatives - delivering measurable value to Source to Pay BPO, Aerospace Leadership, and the Executive Steering Committee.

Technical Debt Reduction

The current Ariba-to-S/4 integration layer for direct procurement relies on custom interfaces that compound maintenance complexity with every platform upgrade. Consolidating into S/4HANA eliminates this customization and aligns to SAP-recommended standard architecture.

Reduced Sustainment Costs

Operating procurement across two platforms compounds support and operational overhead. Consolidation reduces long-term TCO by eliminating dual-platform sustainment, training, and incident resolution workflows.

Process Integrity & Controls

Executing procurement within the ERP core ensures authorization controls, approval workflows, and audit trails are governed by a single system of record - with controls to prevent direct submission in Ariba and indirect submission in S/4.

Operational Continuity

The phased delivery approach with built-in validation checkpoints ensures no procurement process is disrupted without validated alternatives in place. The engagement is structured to maintain business continuity throughout the transition.

For the Source to Pay BPO: This engagement preserves process integrity, ensures auditability, and delivers operational continuity. Every procurement control currently executed in Ariba will be mapped, validated, and operationalized within S/4HANA before go-live.

For Aerospace Leadership: This is a technical debt play. Removing the custom integration layer between Ariba and S/4 for direct procurement reduces the customization footprint, eliminates a redundant platform dependency, and lowers the long-term sustainment burden on EIS.

Section 03

Project Scope & Approach

S/4HANA Direct Procurement Scope

This engagement encompasses the end‑to‑end transition of Direct Procurement processes from SAP Ariba to SAP S/4HANA. The following S/4HANA functional areas are in scope: Direct PR entry in S/4HANA (Fiori/SAP GUI), Direct PR approvals and workflow enablement, PR‑to‑PO creation rules and release strategies, prime contract flow down on PR/PO, WBS assignment on PR/PO, DPAS rating on PR/PO, PR and PO output updates, account assignment and costing, purchasing info records and sourcing record dependencies, supplier contract management, and internal compliance requirements. Controls will be implemented to prevent direct procurement submission in Ariba and indirect procurement submission in S/4.

Dassian Contract Flow Down

The engagement includes Dassian Contract Flow Down implementation covering configuration, documentation, testing, and training. A dedicated Dassian Contract Management resource will support design, build, and testing phases to ensure contract flow down requirements are properly integrated into the S/4HANA direct procurement process.

Integration Scope

The following integrations are in scope for this engagement and will require validation, updates, or reconfiguration to support the transition from Ariba to S/4HANA as the system of execution for direct procurement:

EIS Technical Responsibilities: Aerospace EIS retains technical responsibility for EPIC integration, ServiceNow interface updates, Metrology API updates, AP Essentials validation, and S/4HANA workflow development. RevTech owns SAP Custom Fields and Logic (CFL) configuration on PR and will provide functional requirements, design specifications, and testing support for all EIS-owned technical deliverables.

Training

RevTech will develop updated end-user training content covering the future-state Direct Procurement process in S/4HANA, including Dassian Contract Flow Down. RevTech will deliver end-user training directly to the Source-to-Pay (STP) team and provide training content to the Aerospace Training team for non-STP training needs. A dedicated Training Lead will be engaged part-time during the Build phase and part-time during Full Testing.

Ariba AP Validation

While invoice processing redesign is not in scope, this engagement includes validation that the existing Ariba AP and invoice processing workflows - including three‑way match, invoice verification, and payment execution - continue to function correctly when Purchase Orders and line items originate from S/4HANA rather than Ariba. This validation ensures downstream invoice matching is not disrupted by the procurement transition.

Out of Scope

Redesign of indirect procurement processes (except where UX guidance overlaps), implementation of catalog buying for Direct Procurement in S/4HANA, broad redesign of supplier onboarding or lifecycle management, major enhancements to receiving/inventory beyond PR‑to‑PO and invoice continuity requirements, large‑scale replacement of Ariba B&I invoicing, organizational change management (OCM), and security/basis management are excluded from this engagement.

SAP Ariba Shared Services

Revelation Technologies estimates that Aerospace will require approximately 80 hours of SAP Ariba Shared Services support during this engagement. To offset this cost, RevTech is providing a $5,000 credit applied to the total engagement investment. Coordination with SAP Ariba Shared Services is the responsibility of Aerospace; RevTech will collaborate with the Shared Services team as needed.

Delivery Approach

Revelation Technologies will execute this engagement using a structured, phase‑gated methodology aligned with SAP Activate principles. Under the Firm Fixed Price option, phase gates serve as formal milestone acceptance points. Under T&M, they serve as governance checkpoints. This proposal reflects a primarily remote delivery model with select on‑site support at Aerospace's El Segundo, CA offices as mutually agreed.

TAC‑Furnished Property & Security

RevTech acknowledges that Aerospace will provide necessary business infrastructure including laptops, office space, and network access. No RevTech (or other non‑TAC) laptops or computers will be used in support of deliverables. All data, deliverables, status reports, and information generated for or used on this engagement will remain within Aerospace systems.

Fiscal Freeze Compliance: No project activity will occur during August and September. The delivery timeline is structured to accommodate this constraint, with initial testing completed before the freeze and full testing resuming in October.
