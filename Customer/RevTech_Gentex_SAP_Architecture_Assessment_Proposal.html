Section 01

Executive Summary

Revelation Technologies proposes a structured, three-phase Solution Architecture Assessment of Gentex Corporation's SAP S/4HANA environment to evaluate the current state, identify key design decisions, and deliver a prioritized digital transformation and AI readiness roadmap.

Gentex Corporation has operated its SAP S/4HANA environment for over a year, establishing a solid operational foundation. The emphasis now shifts from stabilization to optimization: getting more out of the existing investment while designing for scale across both commercial production and defense contract compliance requirements.

This engagement delivers an independent, end-to-end review of Gentex's SAP environment across ten functional domains. This is not a health check. It is an architecture assessment grounded in industry-standard best practices across Aerospace & Defense, with a focus on identifying areas where manual processes can be reduced in favor of automation, ensuring Gentex has a clear path toward taking full advantage of its available S/4HANA capabilities, and optimizing for commercial speed and flexibility while making sure defense compliance requirements are met.

The assessment is structured as three consecutive phases, each executed as a three-week sprint followed by a one-week client review period. Each phase builds directly on the outputs of the prior phase, resulting in a digital transformation roadmap that positions Gentex for its next stage of growth.

Engagement Structure: Three phases delivered over 12 weeks. Each phase concludes with a formal deliverable acceptance gate and milestone billing of $76,666.67. Total engagement investment: $230,000.

Implementation Credit: Upon Gentex's selection of Revelation Technologies for future implementation and/or digital transformation work, RevTech will apply a credit of 20% of total assessment billings ($46,000) toward the first milestone of the future engagement.

Revelation Technologies brings deep SAP S/4HANA and Aerospace & Defense domain expertise to this engagement. Our team has executed architecture reviews, procurement transformations, and ERP optimization initiatives for organizations operating under comparable regulatory and compliance frameworks. We understand that Gentex operates across both commercial and defense business lines, and we have structured the assessment to address both dimensions across all workstreams.

Section 03

Assessment Scope - Functional Domains

Each domain will be evaluated from a current-state and future-state lens, with a dual focus: ensuring compliance integrity for defense programs while enabling operational flexibility and optimization for commercial lines of business.

01
Proposal Management, Estimating & Contract Intake
Future-state platform evaluation (TwentyFive IPE, Cognitus, Dassian) and integration with downstream SAP processes. Streamlined commercial bids alongside compliant, auditable defense proposal structures.
02
Cost Accounting & Finance Design
Dual costing architecture - lean cost structures for commercial programs alongside DCAA-compliant indirect rate pools, CAS-aligned cost allocation (if applicable), and auditable financial reporting for defense. Assessment of the intercompany accounting strategy and process, including transfer pricing and evaluation of S/4HANA Advanced Intercompany capabilities.
03
Production Planning & Execution / Supply Chain
MRP, shop floor integration, demand planning, and alignment to Projects/demand networks. Material Master governance and BOM management, including BOM intake processes aligned to industry-standard practices. Roadmap evaluation: SAC and advanced planning capabilities.
04
Logistics, Material Management & Inventory
End-to-end material flow, inventory accuracy, and logistics integration. Flexible warehouse design for commercial velocity; controlled, auditable tracking for defense programs.
05
SAP Project Systems (PS) Design
WBS structure optimization, program management alignment, and earned value reporting. Lightweight structures for commercial; full WBS hierarchy with EVM and IPMR/IPMDAR for defense.
06
Program / Project / Resource Management
Evaluation of solutions such as Cora PPM and how they connect to and enhance the SAP backbone for program execution.
07
Billing, Revenue Recognition & Cash Management
Firm Fixed Price and T&M billing structures, milestone and progress billing, revenue recognition, tax handling, downpayment processing, and cash management. Proper fund control for government contracts where applicable.
08
Time Recording, Labor Costing & Timesheets
Shop floor labor (CATS integration, manufacturing labor confirmations, production time capture) and non-shop floor labor (salaried time entry, indirect labor allocation). Review of the time approval process across both labor types. Simplified time entry for commercial; DCAA-compliant total-time accounting (if applicable), floor-check readiness, segregation of direct/indirect labor charges, and granular, auditable labor tracking for defense.
09
Procurement & Source-to-Pay
End-to-end procurement lifecycle. Optimized catalog-driven purchasing for commercial; FAR/DFAR-compliant workflows (if applicable) with cost-allowability checks for defense.
10
Available-to-Promise (ATP) & Order Fulfillment
Evaluation of SAP S/4HANA ATP capabilities including basic ATP, product allocation, and advanced ATP (aATP). Assessment of how ATP rules can be tuned for rapid commercial order confirmation while respecting priority allocation for defense fulfillment schedules.

Proposed Phase Deliverables

Each phase produces defined deliverables that feed the next. Formal acceptance of phase deliverables constitutes the milestone trigger for payment.

Phase 01 Current State Assessment

Deliverables

  • Current State Assessment Report - Per-workstream evaluation of the existing S/4HANA environment across all 10 scope domains, each assessed through the four-lens framework (People & Personas, Process, Technology, Data Integration), with findings segmented by impact on commercial operations versus defense compliance posture.
  • Key Findings & Opportunity Catalog - Consolidated catalog of findings and optimization opportunities per workstream, focused on where the current solution can be improved, where manual processes can be replaced with automation, and where available S/4HANA capabilities are not yet being used. Prioritized and tagged by business segment (commercial/defense).
  • Draft KDD List - Preliminary identification of Key Design Decisions surfaced during the current state assessment, organized by workstream. Gives Phase 2 a head start on which decisions need to be evaluated and resolved.
  • Phase 1 Executive Readout - Stakeholder presentation summarizing current-state findings, optimization opportunities, and draft KDD list for leadership alignment before entering Phase 2.

Milestone Payment: $76,666.67 upon Gentex acceptance of Phase 1 deliverables.

Phase 02 KDD Assessment & Solution Options

Deliverables

  • KDD Workbook by Workstream - Building on the Phase 1 draft KDD list, a fully developed set of Key Design Decisions per workstream documenting: the finding or opportunity from Phase 1, solution options evaluated (including SAP standard capabilities, ATP re-design/architecture, third-party platforms such as TwentyFive IPE, Cognitus, Dassian, Cora PPM where applicable), pros/cons, compliance implications, and RevTech's recommended option with rationale.
  • Commercial & Defense Design Callout Catalog - A targeted catalog highlighting areas where Gentex's current design could be rethought to better accommodate the flexibility and throughput demands of the commercial business and/or deliver a more elegant, streamlined solution to meet compliance requirements (DCAA, CAS, FAR/DFARS, if applicable) for defense programs. Each callout includes the current design pattern, the limitation it introduces, and the recommended design direction.
  • Platform Evaluation Recommendations - Independent fit analysis of third-party platforms where relevant, with integration considerations and total cost of ownership perspective.
  • Future State Solution Architecture Blueprint - Architecture design across People, Process, Technology, and Data, built on the KDD decisions, designed to scale across both commercial optimization and defense compliance.
  • Phase 2 Executive Readout - Presentation of KDD outcomes, architecture blueprint, commercial/defense design callouts, and recommended solution direction for stakeholder alignment before entering Phase 3.

Milestone Payment: $76,666.67 upon Gentex acceptance of Phase 2 deliverables.

Phase 03 Digital Transformation & AI Readiness Roadmap

Deliverables

  • Digital Transformation Roadmap - A sequenced, time-phased implementation plan organized by workstream, prioritized by effort, business impact, and compliance urgency. Distinguishes quick wins for commercial efficiency from must-haves for defense audit readiness. Includes dependency mapping between workstreams, resource/skill requirements, and estimated level of effort per initiative.
  • Implementation Wave Plan - Logical grouping of roadmap items into implementation waves/releases, with recommended sequencing, duration estimates, and team composition for each wave.
  • AI Readiness Assessment & Use Case Roadmap - Full AI readiness evaluation across process, data, and systems dimensions, accompanied by a prioritized roadmap of high-value AI use cases (demand forecasting, intelligent MRP, automated compliance checking, predictive maintenance, contract risk analysis) with feasibility ratings, data prerequisites, platform dependencies, expected ROI, and sequenced initiatives from quick wins to longer-horizon items.
  • Business Case & Investment Summary - High-level cost/benefit framework for the recommended roadmap to support Gentex leadership decision-making and budget planning.
  • Final Executive Readout & Recommendations Presentation - Stakeholder-ready presentation covering the full picture: current state findings, architecture vision, KDD decisions, the prioritized transformation roadmap, and AI readiness with recommended next steps.

Milestone Payment: $76,666.67 upon Gentex acceptance of Phase 3 deliverables.

Section 08

Risks & Mitigations

Risk

SME Availability

Delayed access to Gentex subject matter experts could compress sprint timelines, particularly during discovery-intensive Phase 1.

Mitigation

Pre-Engagement Scheduling

SME requirements and interview schedules will be defined during kickoff and tracked weekly. RevTech will provide interview guides in advance to maximize session efficiency.

Risk

Undocumented Configuration

Custom configurations, workarounds, or non-standard processes not captured in existing documentation may extend discovery timelines.

Mitigation

System-Level Discovery

RevTech will supplement SME interviews with direct system analysis to identify configuration that may not be reflected in documentation. Scope-expanding discoveries are escalated via change control.

Risk

Stakeholder Alignment Delays

Multiple stakeholders across commercial and defense business units may require additional time to align on KDD decisions and architecture direction.

Mitigation

Structured Review Gates

Each phase includes a dedicated one-week review period specifically for stakeholder alignment. Executive readouts at each gate ensure leadership visibility and decision authority.

Risk

Data Access Constraints

Security or access restrictions on production data or system environments may limit the depth of current-state analysis.

Mitigation

Early Access Planning

System access requirements will be defined during kickoff. RevTech does not need access to live production data, but will need to see data that is representative of real data (anonymized or masked datasets are acceptable) to ensure the assessment reflects actual operating conditions.
