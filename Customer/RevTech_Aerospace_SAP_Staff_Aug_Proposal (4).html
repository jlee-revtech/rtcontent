Background
Engagement Context & Current State
SAP S/4HANA Go-Live & Hypercare
The Aerospace Corporation went live on their SAP S/4HANA implementation in October 2025 and has been operating under a hypercare support model for the past six months. That hypercare period is now concluding, and Aerospace is transitioning into a long-term sustainment model.
Long-Term Sustainment Direction
Aerospace has indicated their intent to bring sustainment capabilities in-house over time. However, they have identified near-term gaps in internal skills, institutional knowledge, and capacity required to sustain the new solution independently. Aerospace has asked RevTech to provide staff augmentation support to bridge those gaps during this critical post-go-live period.
Unified Rate Deck
Aerospace recently requested a single, consolidated rate structure that eliminates the distinction between sustainment break/fix work and sustainment grow/evolve work. This proposal delivers that unified rate deck, simplifying procurement and enabling Aerospace to deploy RevTech resources flexibly across both operational support and incremental improvement activities under one agreement.
Grow/Evolve Projects — Separate SOWs
It is assumed that discrete, scoped grow/evolve project initiatives (e.g., Direct Supply Chain redirect to S/4HANA) will be contracted under separate Statements of Work with defined deliverables and pricing. This proposal covers the staff augmentation and sustainment support model only.

Section 02
Engagement Structure & Value Proposition
🌟

7% Discount Activation

Aerospace commits to a minimum of three (3) RevTech sustainment-level resources. In return, all Elevated and Consulting delivery rates are reduced by 7%, unlocking the discounted rate column across both tables.

🔁

Built-In Reach-Back Support

Each sustainment resource includes 10 hours per month of reach-back access to Elevated-level consultants at no additional cost. This ensures continuity and expert guidance without incremental billing. Elevated resources carry a 2-business-day response SLA on all reach-back requests, unless an alternative timeline is mutually agreed upon by Aerospace and RevTech.

📚

60-Day Transition & KT Period

Aerospace agrees to a 60-day onboarding window during which sustainment resources receive knowledge transfer and mentorship from Elevated resources. This period begins once sustainment resources are committed.

📈

Elevated Blocks (200hr Min)

Aerospace may purchase Elevated sustainment resources in minimum 200-hour blocks. Hours above the 200-hour minimum can be purchased as needed, providing flexible access to senior-level expertise.

📅

1-Year Rate Lock & Annual Review

All rates in this proposal are locked for a 12-month period (April 2026 through April 2027). Rates are subject to annual review thereafter, with ceilings on rate uplifts based on market conditions and factors to be defined in the Staff Augmentation / Sustainment MSA. Resource adjustment cadence established after the initial 60-day onboarding period.

👥

Resource Interview & Approval

Aerospace retains the right to interview and approve all staff augmentation resources prior to onboarding. This ensures each candidate meets Aerospace's standards for technical competency, domain relevance, and culture alignment before being assigned to the account. All resources onboarded to the Aerospace account must be verified U.S. Citizens.

🤝

Aligned Incentives, Lower Cost

This model incentivizes both parties toward the sustainment tier, which carries a meaningfully lower cost for Aerospace and creates long-term stability for RevTech. By anchoring the engagement around sustainment resources rather than premium consulting hours, both organizations benefit from a model built for enduring value rather than short-term project dependency.

⭐
Strategic Partnership & Market Reference
RevTech values this engagement not only as a delivery commitment but as a strategic partnership. As part of this proposal, RevTech respectfully requests that The Aerospace Corporation serve as a referenceable client in the market upon successful execution of this agreement. Aerospace's reputation as a premier FFRDC and national security space leader carries significant weight, and RevTech is committed to earning that endorsement through sustained, high-quality delivery.
