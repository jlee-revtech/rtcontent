Executive Context
Following our conversation at SAP Aerospace & Defense Innovation Days (SAP ADID), this document serves as a structured playback of what the RevTech team heard from the Gentex team regarding your SAP S/4HANA environment, your current state, and where you are looking to go. This outline is intended to confirm alignment on scope and intent before we move into a formal engagement proposal. Please review, correct, or refine anything that does not accurately reflect our discussion.
01 What We Heard

Based on our conversation at SAP ADID, RevTech's understanding of Gentex's current position and strategic direction is captured below. This represents our good-faith playback — not a contractual commitment — and is subject to your review and correction.

Where You Are Today
Maturing SAP Foundation
Gentex has an operational SAP S/4HANA environment in place with a solid foundation. The emphasis now is on extracting maximum value from that investment and designing for scale — balancing the agility and throughput demands of commercial production with the compliance rigor required by defense contracts.
What You're Looking For
Independent End-to-End Review
An objective, end-to-end review of the current environment — not just a health check, but a forward-looking architecture perspective grounded in industry-standard best practices across A&D, optimizing for commercial speed and flexibility while ensuring every defense compliance requirement is met without compromise.
Overarching Objective: A holistic Solution Architecture Review spanning People & Personas, Process, Technology, and Data Integration — not just what the system does today, but how it should be designed to scale with Gentex across both commercial and defense lines of business. The architecture must be flexible enough to maximize throughput and efficiency on the commercial side while maintaining full compliance posture (DCAA, DFARS, CAS, FAR/DFAR) on the defense side.
02 Proposed Scope — Full Solution Architecture Review

The following eleven functional domains represent the scope of the Solution Architecture Review as discussed. Each domain will be evaluated from a current-state and future-state lens, with a dual focus: ensuring compliance integrity for defense programs while enabling operational flexibility and optimization for commercial lines of business. This balance is central to every recommendation.

01
Proposal Management, Estimating & Contract Intake
Including future-state platform evaluation — TwentyFive IPE, Cognitus, and/or Dassian — and integration with downstream SAP processes. Streamlined estimating workflows for commercial bids alongside compliant, auditable proposal structures for defense contracts.
02
Cost Accounting & Finance Design
Dual costing architecture — lean, throughput-oriented cost structures for commercial programs alongside DCAA-compliant indirect rate pools, CAS-aligned cost allocation, and auditable financial reporting for defense contracts.
03
Production Planning & Execution / Supply Chain
MRP, shop floor integration, and demand planning, including alignment to Projects / demand networks. Optimized for high-volume commercial throughput while supporting traceability and lot control requirements on defense programs. Forward-looking roadmap evaluation: SAC and advanced planning capabilities within S/4HANA.
04
Logistics, Material Management & Inventory
End-to-end material flow, inventory accuracy, and logistics integration within the S/4HANA environment — flexible warehouse design for commercial velocity alongside controlled, auditable material segregation and tracking for defense programs.
05
SAP Project Systems (PS) Design
WBS structure optimization, potential future program management alignment, and earned value reporting architecture. Lightweight project structures for commercial work; full WBS hierarchy with earned value management and IPMR/IPMDAR support for defense contracts.
06
Program / Project / Resource Management Integration
Including evaluation of solutions such as Cora PPM and how they connect to and enhance the SAP backbone for program execution.
07
Billing, Revenue Recognition & Cash Management
Contract billing design, revenue recognition compliance, and treasury / cash management integration within S/4HANA. Streamlined milestone and progress billing for commercial contracts; compliant cost-plus, T&M, and CPFF billing structures with proper fund control for government contracts.
08
Time Recording & Labor Costing Design
Labor capture, cost allocation, and timekeeping design — simplified time entry for commercial operations while enforcing DCAA-compliant total-time accounting, floor-check readiness, and segregation of direct/indirect labor charges on defense programs.
09
Timesheets & Manufacturing Labor
Cross-Application Time Sheet (CATS) integration, manufacturing labor confirmation workflows, shop floor time capture, and alignment of production labor postings with cost objects and project structures in S/4HANA. Efficient bulk confirmation for commercial production lines; granular, auditable labor tracking by cost object for defense programs to support incurred-cost submissions.
10
Procurement & Source-to-Pay
End-to-end procurement lifecycle in S/4HANA — purchase requisitions, purchase orders, supplier management, contract purchasing, goods receipt, and invoice verification. Optimized catalog-driven purchasing for commercial efficiency; controlled, FAR/DFAR-compliant procurement workflows with cost-allowability checks, adequate price competition documentation, and consent-to-subcontract support for defense programs.
11
Available-to-Promise (ATP) & Order Fulfillment
Evaluation of SAP S/4HANA ATP capabilities — including basic ATP, product allocation, and advanced ATP (aATP) with backorder processing. Assessment of how ATP rules can be tuned for rapid commercial order confirmation and delivery commitment while respecting priority allocation and controlled release logic for defense program fulfillment schedules.
03 Solution Architecture Review Framework

Each of the eleven scope areas above will be evaluated through RevTech's four-lens Solution Architecture framework. This ensures that findings are actionable across all dimensions of enterprise transformation — not limited to system configuration alone. In every lens, we will distinguish between what must be optimized for commercial agility and what must be hardened for defense compliance, delivering an architecture that serves both without forcing unnecessary overhead on either. RevTech's evaluations are benchmarked against industry-standard practices observed across leading A&D organizations — ensuring recommendations reflect what works at scale in the sector, not just theoretical best practice.

👥
People & Personas
User roles, access design, organizational alignment, and change readiness — including role differentiation between commercial and defense program teams
⚙️
Process
End-to-end process flows designed for commercial throughput, with compliance control points embedded where defense programs require them
🔧
Technology
SAP configuration, ecosystem tools, integration architecture, and forward-looking roadmap — leveraging a shared platform backbone with configurable controls by business segment
🔗
Data Integration
Data flows, master data governance, integration design, and reporting / analytics foundation — with clear segmentation to support both commercial KPIs and defense audit trails
04 AI Readiness Assessment & Use Case Roadmap

As part of the Solution Architecture Review, RevTech will conduct a dedicated AI Readiness Assessment across three dimensions — process, data, and systems — to evaluate Gentex's current posture and chart a pragmatic path toward high-value AI adoption. This assessment is grounded in RevTech's industry-standard perspective across aerospace & defense, drawing on patterns we see working at scale across the sector — not theoretical possibilities, but proven approaches already delivering ROI in comparable A&D environments.

AI-1
Process Readiness
Evaluation of current business processes for AI augmentation potential — identifying where manual effort, decision latency, or repetitive pattern recognition creates opportunity. On the commercial side, focus on throughput acceleration and predictive operations; on the defense side, focus on compliance automation and audit-trail generation without introducing risk.
AI-2
Data Readiness
Assessment of data quality, completeness, accessibility, and governance maturity across SAP and adjacent systems. AI solutions are only as strong as the data they consume — this evaluation identifies gaps in master data, transactional data pipelines, and data integration architecture that must be addressed before or alongside AI deployment.
AI-3
Systems & Platform Readiness
Review of the current technology landscape for AI enablement — SAP BTP, embedded AI capabilities in S/4HANA, integration architecture, and third-party AI/ML platform fit. Evaluation of what can be activated within the existing stack versus what requires new investment, informed by industry-standard reference architectures in the A&D sector.
AI-4
High-Value Use Case Roadmap
Identification and prioritization of AI use cases mapped to Gentex's specific business context — from demand forecasting and intelligent MRP to automated compliance checking, predictive maintenance, and contract risk analysis. Each use case will be evaluated for feasibility, data readiness, expected ROI, and implementation complexity, delivered as a sequenced roadmap with clear quick wins and longer-horizon initiatives.
RevTech's Perspective: Our recommendations are anchored in what we see working across the aerospace & defense industry — not vendor marketing. We bring an independent, industry-standard lens shaped by real implementations across A&D organizations of similar scale and complexity, ensuring Gentex benefits from proven patterns rather than experimental approaches.

  • Future State Solution Architecture Blueprint A forward-looking architecture design recommendation covering People, Process, Technology, and Data — designed to scale with Gentex's production and contract growth while maintaining a unified platform that flexes between commercial optimization and defense compliance requirements.

  • AI Readiness Assessment & Use Case Roadmap A dedicated report covering process, data, and systems readiness for AI adoption — accompanied by a prioritized roadmap of high-value AI use cases with feasibility ratings, data prerequisites, and expected ROI, grounded in industry-standard patterns from across the A&D sector.
