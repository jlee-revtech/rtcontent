C12 Peer Advisory

Key Questions for the Group

Two strategic challenges I'm bringing this quarter. Not looking for validation. Looking for the perspective I can't see from inside.

01
Organizational Development  ·  30 Days

How do I let go of control and build genuine trust with my leadership team?

The Situation
  • I work in the business more than on it
  • I direct and control because I don't fully trust my team to execute at my expected level
  • Creates a bottleneck. Key decisions flow through me, limiting scale.
  • We run EOS but I'm not fully embracing it
  • I recognize this is as much a personal growth issue as an organizational one
Desired Outcome
  • Leadership team that executes without my constant involvement
  • Clear accountability structures and empowered leaders
  • Transition from working in the business to working on it
  • A business that can scale beyond what I can personally manage
Proposed Solutions
1

Work with a coach to identify trust/control triggers and build a personal delegation plan

2

Use EOS tools more rigorously. Accountability Chart, Rocks, L10s become the checkpoint instead of me.

3

Invest in structured 1-on-1s and leader development. Build confidence through demonstrated results, not assumptions.

02
Revenue Generation  ·  90 Days

How do we build a sustainable sales engine and reduce dependence on any single client?

The Situation
  • RevTech grew entirely on referrals and relationships. No formal sales or marketing.
  • One client (Aerospace Corp) drove 73% of revenue over the past two years
  • That engagement concluded at end of 2025
  • Deal cycles are long. Pipeline for 2027 needs to be built now.
  • The market opportunity is real; we lack the infrastructure to capitalize on it consistently
Desired Outcome
  • Repeatable, scalable sales process generating consistent inbound and outbound leads
  • No single client exceeds 40% of revenue
  • $45M in consistent year-over-year revenue within 5 years
  • Sales infrastructure that doesn't depend on me personally
Proposed Solutions
1

Hire a dedicated BD professional within 90 days and build a formal sales playbook with defined stages and metrics

2

Launch targeted content and thought leadership strategy leveraging our GovCon/A&D niche positioning

3

Pursue direct client engagements vs. subcontracting; add sustainment and training as recurring revenue streams
