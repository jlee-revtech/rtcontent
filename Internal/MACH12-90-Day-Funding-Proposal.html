01 // Executive Summary

FROM CUSTOM SOLUTIONS TO SCALABLE PRODUCTS

Revelation Technologies has spent years embedded inside the most complex ERP environments in the US Government Contracting and Aerospace & Defense industry. In that time, we haven’t just consulted. We’ve built. Custom tools, accelerators, workarounds, and solutions that our clients needed because nothing on the market addressed their specific compliance, reporting, and operational requirements.

The pattern is unmistakable. Across engagements (FAR/DFARS cost compliance, EVMS reporting, WBS-driven program management), RevTech consultants have repeatedly designed and deployed custom solutions to fill the same gaps. These solutions work. Clients depend on them. But they live as one-off deliverables, rebuilt and re-scoped at every new engagement.

Mach12.ai exists to change that equation. Instead of rebuilding custom solutions engagement by engagement, we productize the most commonly deployed patterns into AI-powered software that can be delivered at scale and at an aggressive pace. We already know what the market needs because we’ve been building it by hand for years. Now, with AI as the accelerant, we can ship in weeks what used to take months, and serve the entire market instead of one client at a time.

This isn’t speculative product development. This is a consultancy with deep domain authority operationalizing its own institutional knowledge into software. No other AI company entering this space has that foundation.

02 // Market Focus

TWO MARKETS, ONE PLATFORM

Mach12.ai serves two distinct but reinforcing markets. External products target GovCon and A&D buyers directly, as well as professional services companies beyond the SAP ecosystem. Internal products accelerate and differentiate RevTech’s consulting delivery, creating an intercompany revenue stream that offsets startup costs while stress-testing products in live client environments.

External Market

GovCon, A&D & Professional Services

AI-powered software for US Government Contractors, Aerospace & Defense companies ($750M–$3B revenue), and professional services firms. While Mach12.ai products naturally integrate with SAP, they are not exclusive to SAP, opening the addressable market to any organization running complex project-driven operations regardless of ERP platform.

‣ AI Contract Analytics
‣ EVMS Intelligence Suite
‣ Predictive Cost & Schedule
‣ Compliance Automation Engine
Internal Market

RevTech Delivery Acceleration

Tools built by Mach12.ai that RevTech consultants use to deliver faster, differentiate from competitors, and demonstrate AI-augmented consulting in live engagements. RevTech pays Mach12.ai through an intercompany licensing arrangement, creating revenue that offsets initial startup costs.

‣ Diagrams App (piloting at Boeing)
‣ Tesseract PPM Platform
‣ Consultant Productivity Suite
‣ Client Reporting Accelerators

Intercompany Revenue Model: RevTech will license Mach12.ai’s internal tools through a formal intercompany agreement, creating a recurring revenue stream that offsets Mach12.ai’s startup costs. RevTech receives differentiated tooling that improves delivery margins and win rates. Both entities benefit, and the revenue is structured to reflect fair market value for the software provided.

03 // Business Model

THREE PILLARS OF REVENUE

Across both markets, Mach12.ai operates three distinct business models, each targeting different buyer needs, creating multiple entry points into the ecosystem, and expanding RevTech’s reach into new customers and industries.

◆

Custom AI Solutions

Bespoke AI-powered software built directly to mission requirements. Your problem, your data, your competitive advantage. High-touch, high-margin engagements for complex use cases that off-the-shelf tools can’t address.

◊

Partner-Ready Platforms

Pre-built AI modules designed for software partners to integrate into existing platforms. Accelerate roadmaps without building from scratch. Recurring license revenue at scale.

■

Client Delivery & Acquisition Tools

Free-to-client applications that accelerate project delivery and demonstrate immediate value. These tools open doors to new customers, including organizations outside the SAP ecosystem, giving RevTech a broader footprint and a natural on-ramp to deeper consulting engagements.

Platform Independence: Mach12.ai products are designed with native SAP integration where applicable, drawing on RevTech’s deep SAP expertise, but are not exclusive to SAP. This deliberate architectural decision expands the addressable market beyond RevTech’s traditional SAP client base, positioning Mach12.ai as a gateway to new customers, new industries, and new consulting opportunities that RevTech would not otherwise access.

07 // Risk & Mitigation

KEY RISK FACTORS

Mitigated

Domain Credibility

Mach12.ai launches from inside an established GovCon consultancy. RevTech’s existing client relationships, domain authority, and years of custom solution delivery de-risk market entry and product-market fit.

Mitigated

Product Validation Risk

Internal tools deployed to RevTech consultants create a built-in validation loop. Products are tested in real client environments before external launch, reducing the risk of building something the market doesn’t want.

Mitigated

Revenue Runway

The intercompany licensing model generates revenue from Day 1 of tool deployment, reducing dependence on external sales cycles that can run 6–12 months in GovCon.

Mitigated

Market Concentration

Platform-independent architecture (SAP-native but not SAP-exclusive) and free client-facing tools expand the addressable market beyond RevTech’s traditional SAP base into new industries and customer segments.

Monitor

Founder Time Dilution

At 25% allocation, Josh maintains RevTech obligations while providing strategic direction. Keith and Ryan provide executive oversight to ensure alignment and accountability across both entities.

Monitor

Single Contractor Dependency

Development velocity relies on one full-time contractor. Mitigation: document architecture from day one, maintain clean codebase standards, and identify backup resources from the RevTech network.

08 // Strategic Advantage

WHY MACH12.AI WINS

Every AI startup entering the GovCon space is technology-first, domain-second. They build generic tools and hope defense contractors adapt. Mach12.ai is the inverse. We start with the solutions we’ve already built by hand, validated in production, and depended on by clients, then apply AI to deliver them at scale.

RevTech consultants have spent years inside FAR/DFARS compliance environments, CAS implementations, EVMS reporting structures, and S/4HANA transformations. In every engagement, they’ve had to build custom tools because the market doesn’t offer what these organizations actually need. Those custom builds are the product roadmap. The institutional knowledge is the moat.

The dual-market structure compounds this advantage. Free client-facing tools open doors to organizations RevTech has never reached, including companies outside the SAP ecosystem entirely. Internal tools harden products in real client environments. And the intercompany revenue they generate funds external product development. Each cycle reinforces the next.

This $114K gross investment is not speculative R&D. It’s a focused, 90-day sprint to operationalize institutional knowledge that already exists, validate products with users who are already engaged, and open new markets through tools that demonstrate immediate value. The team is in place. The domain expertise is proven. The brand is built. This proposal funds execution.
